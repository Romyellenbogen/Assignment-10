Female musicians I've seen in concert

By Romy Ellenbogen

I learned how to create responsive effects with JQuery. The images and buttons on this page should be responsive. By clicking, you can see photos of the artists, more information about them, and my favorite songs.

Japanese Breakfast

Image source

Japanese Breakfast is the brainchild of Michelle Zauner. She has released two albums so far, Psychopomp and Soft Sounds From Another Planet. Zauner is not Japanese, but is of Korean and Jewish descent and grew up in Philadelphia and Eugene, Oregon.

Till Death

Lorde

Image source

Lorde is the stage name for Ella Yelich-O'Connor, a young woman from New Zealand. She has released one EP and two full length albums, both of which have been met with commercial success. At 17, she was the youngest person to top the US Billboard 100 since 1987.

Ribs

Wolf Alice

Image source

Wolf Alice is a four-piece band from London, England. They have had the same four members since 2012, with Ellie Rowsell leading the group as the main vocal and a guitar player. They have released two full length albums.

Bros

Mitski

Image source

Mitski Miyawaki is a Japanese-American singer who plays the guitar and the piano. She grew up across the nation and lived in 13 different countries. She studied music at Purchase College and has released five albums, with her latest release, Be The Cowboy, topping college radio play.
